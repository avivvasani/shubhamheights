About Shubham Heights

Shubham Heights is not just a residence; it's a statement of contemporary living. Offering meticulously designed 2 & 3 BHK luxurious flats, our project promises a lifestyle defined by sophistication, comfort, and tranquility. Every detail, from the grand entrance to the smallest fixture, reflects a commitment to quality and aesthetic excellence.

Nestled in a vibrant yet peaceful locale, Shubham Heights offers the perfect balance between urban convenience and a serene environment. Experience the joy of spacious interiors, modern amenities, and a community that feels like family. Your journey to an elevated lifestyle begins here.

Premium Specifications

Structure & Walls

  • RCC frame structure for enhanced durability.
  • External 6" and internal 4" thick brickwork.
  • Double coat external plaster with acrylic emulsion paint.
  • Internal Neru finished with Premium Emulsion Paint.

Flooring & Finishes

  • High-quality Vitrified tiles flooring with 3" skirting.
  • Elegant Granite kitchen platform with stainless steel sink.
  • Glazed tiles dado up to slab level above kitchen platform.
  • Ceramic flooring and glazed tile dado upto Lintel level in toilets.

Electrical & Plumbing

  • ISI Mark Concealed electrification and plumbing throughout.
  • Dedicated Dish TV cable point in the living room.
  • Convenient Geyser points provided in all bathrooms.
  • Provision for inverter wiring for uninterrupted power.
  • Ample light points, fan points, and plug points in each room.

Doors & Windows

  • All doors are laminate finish Flush doors with sturdy wooden frames.
  • Elegant Toilet door frames crafted from Granite.
  • Three track coated Aluminum sliding windows with safety grill.
  • Durable Granite frames in all windows.

Amenities & Facilities

  • Allotted Car Parking with durable chequered tiles/paver blocks.
  • Grand Decorative entrance lobby.
  • Power Backup for lift and common landing areas.
  • Solar lighting in common areas, parking, lift, and for water pump.
  • Comprehensive CCTV Camera Surveillance in lift and parking area.
  • Reliable Municipal & Borewell water Connection.
  • Lush NMC Open space area (Garden) in front of the building.
